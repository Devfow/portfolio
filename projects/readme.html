This website serves as a portfolio for Devyn Fowler, a Designer and Coder.

It showcases various web based projects and works, presented in a nostalgic interface.

Explore the desktop icons to launch different applications, most of these projects were made to showcase newly explored or otherwise abandoned concepts in web design.

Contact Info: Devfow@gmail.com

Balatro-Like Game A simplified web-based interpretation of *Balatro*, built around poker-hand mechanics. Players score points by building hands, progressing through increasingly difficult levels with stackable upgrades — a procedural meditation on chance and progression.

Datascape A hypertext-driven atmospheric experience inspired by Mark Amerika’s *GRAMMATRON*. Presented as a nonlinear digital space that encourages exploration of fractured meaning and immersive ambiguity.

Depressing Adventure A web-based text narrative focusing on a protagonist experiencing extreme depression. Intentionally sparse and bleak, it offers a raw, unfiltered dive into emotional stasis and existential fatigue.

Flesh Syzygy A top-down roguelike dungeon crawler with procedural level generation. Players navigate using arrow keys through surreal environments infused with decay.

Synaptic Decay A compact yet philosophically ambitious text adventure where player character actions are dynamically altered based on previously triggered emotional states. Explores memory, manipulation, and fractured agency in interactive storytelling.
